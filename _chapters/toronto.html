About

The Toronto meetup had our first meetup last October and is still getting its feet! We currently aim to meet every 2nd week (will increase with sufficient interest), we've had a great turn out in our first few meetups and look forward to continuing to grow! This is a great time to join, get to know everyone, and volunteer to run a session!
